Breakfast Recipes

Upma

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Semolina
  • 1/4 cup Peas
  • 1/4 cup Carrot (finely chopped)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Green chilies (slit)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
Preparation Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan; add onions and chilies.
  2. Sauté until golden.
  3. Add peas and carrots; cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add semolina and roast for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add water and salt; stir well.
  6. Cover and cook until water is absorbed.

Lunch Recipes

Andhra Chicken Curry

Ingredients:
  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 Green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp Oil
Preparation Steps:
  1. Heat oil; sauté onions and chilies.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste; cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, spices, and salt; cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken; cover and cook for 20-25 minutes.

Dinner Recipes

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2 cups Mixed vegetables
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 2 Green chilies (slit)
  • 2 tbsp Biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
Preparation Steps:
  1. Rinse and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil; sauté onions and chilies.
  3. Add vegetables and biryani masala; cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add rice and water; boil, then simmer until done.
